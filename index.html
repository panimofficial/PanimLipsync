What's New in V2.0

The biggest update yet. Tap any tab to explore the new features.

Allosaurus Engine

Whisper and Allosaurus now side by side. Whisper estimates phonemes from speech-to-text. Allosaurus detects them directly from audio acoustics, more precise, and supports 2,000+ languages.

  • Whisper for clear speech in common languages
  • Allosaurus for direct acoustic detection
  • Install one or both, switch anytime
  • Works across all three animation modes

Binary Dominant Viseme

Only the dominant viseme gets set to 1. Everything else drops to 0. A hard switch between mouth shapes that gives a clean, snappy 2D-style animation feel, great for stylized or cartoon characters.

Available for Shape Keys and Bone modes. Can be toggled per-project in Processing Options.

Post Processing

A dedicated panel handles Butterworth filter smoothing for softer keyframe curves, and animation transfer (copy, paste) between compatible characters.

Zero extra setup needed. Apply smoothing and transfer directly after generating your animation.

Eye Blinks

Randomized blink animations generated automatically across all animation modes. No manual keyframing, no extra setup.

The Eye Blinks panel lets you control blink frequency and intensity to match your character's personality.

Refinement Panel

After generating, navigate and inspect each phoneme keyframe individually. Replace, reassign, or remove specific mouth shapes without re-processing the full audio.

Available for Shape Keys mode only.

Works with your rig.

Shape keys, bone actions, or ARKit blendshapes, Panim adapts to whatever your character uses.

Three steps.

From raw audio to fully animated mouth, done in minutes, or even seconds.

Load Audio

Import MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A, or AAC. Works in any language with no extra configuration.

Process

Panim detects phonemes locally using your chosen engine and maps them to your rig's visemes.

Animate

Shape keys or bone actions get keyframed automatically. Refine individual frames as needed.

What you need.

Panim runs entirely on your machine. No cloud service, no internet required after the initial model download.

System

  • Blender 4.1 to 5.0
  • RAM 8GB+ recommended (16GB+ for Whisper Large)
  • Storage ~5GB for models and dependencies
  • Windows VC++ 2015–2022 Redistributable x64
  • Mac Limited support, not actively tested

Engines

  • Whisper ~74MB–2.9GB depending on model size
  • Allosaurus ~100MB fixed model
  • Internet Only needed once to download the model
  • GPU CUDA and Metal accelerated automatically if available
  • Audio MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A, AAC

Compatibility Notes

Mac support is limited and not actively tested. Windows is the primary supported platform.

Windows requires a one-time Blender restart after installing dependencies.

Internet is only required once per engine to download the AI model. After that, everything runs locally.

Refinement Panel is available for Shape Keys mode only, not for Bone Actions or ARKit.
